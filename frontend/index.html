    About Resume Analyzer

    Helping job seekers optimize their resumes for better opportunities

    Resume Analyzer uses advanced natural language processing to compare your resume against job descriptions. Our tool identifies matching skills and highlights missing qualifications to help you tailor your resume for specific positions.

    The system uses pattern matching and text similarity algorithms to extract skills from both documents and calculate a match percentage. By understanding exactly what employers are looking for, you can customize your application materials to increase your chances of getting an interview.
